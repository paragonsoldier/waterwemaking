# About

Hello World! I'm Andrew; a 30-something, Christian dad who loves constantly exploring new hobbies.  

When I was in daycare I saw others chaining yarn with their fingers. Later in life, my grandparents took me to visit my aunt. Getting bored, I spotted a basket of yarn and started chaining. My aunt informed me I was crocheting. "I'm what?", I said. She gave me my first crochet hook and proceeded to teach me how to single crochet. From there I've learned many other stiches, started exploring new hobbies, and desired to share this knowledge with others.

In High School, we had a broadcast news show where I hosted a weekly segment named Andrew's Craft Corner.

[Andrew's Craft Corner - YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YXCnfdJ8tlQ&list=PLz1WzJfB77mEp9LSlVRc4V3Z9IcNXXCUk)

After graduation I continued Andrew's Craft Corner by blogging.

[Andrew's Craft Corner - Blog](https://andrewscraftcorner.blogspot.com/)

The blog hasn't been updated since 2011, but I've never stopped making.

Water We Making is the next phase of this journey. In addition to needlecrafts I've been learning balloon animals, woodworking, Amateur Ham Radio, Japanese, Programming and continue to explore new hobbies. This site is here to showcase what I've been making, teach others, and build a community.

Thank you for visiting!
